01Introduction

Welcome to Winova ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our iOS application. By using Winova, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

02Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when using Winova, including:

  • Account information — email address and password (if you create an account)
  • Wins and entries — the daily wins, notes, categories, and moods you log
  • Streak and activity data — your usage patterns, streaks, and engagement history
  • Device information — device type, operating system version, and app version for technical support
  • Purchase history — confirmation of in-app purchases to unlock features
We do not collect location data, contacts, camera access, or any sensitive device permissions beyond what is necessary to operate the app.

03How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Winova app experience
  • Display your wins, streaks, and personal growth progress
  • Sync your data across your devices when signed in
  • Send you notifications you've opted into (e.g., daily reminders)
  • Process in-app purchases and manage your purchase history
  • Respond to your questions and support requests
We do not sell your personal data or use it for targeted advertising.

04Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Base44, our backend infrastructure provider. We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, loss, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

05In-App Purchases

Winova offers in-app purchases processed through Apple's App Store. We do not collect or store your payment information — all transactions are handled directly by Apple. For questions about billing, refunds, or purchase history, please refer to Apple's support at apple.com/support.

We may receive confirmation from Apple that a purchase was completed in order to unlock the relevant features in the app.

06Children's Privacy

Winova is available to users of all ages, including children under 13. We are committed to complying with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information without your consent, please contact us at winova.app1@gmail.com and we will promptly delete such information.

07Sharing of Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties except in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers — we use Base44 as our backend infrastructure provider, which processes data on our behalf under strict confidentiality obligations
  • Legal requirements — if required by law, court order, or governmental authority
  • Protection of rights — to protect the rights, property, or safety of Winova, our users, or others

08Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt out of notifications at any time via your device settings

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at winova.app1@gmail.com.

09Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal information within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.
